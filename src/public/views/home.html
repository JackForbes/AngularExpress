Listing and managing rentals has never been easier.

Throughout the year, thousands of students look for short-term housing — from single beds to entire spaces — for school terms and internships throughout the United States and Canada. With PadPiper’s intuitive design, listing a rental is both transparent and simple. Students on PadPiper offer steady demand, less risk, and higher profit.

Become a PadPiper Landlord

Create and manage listings at no cost to you.

With a step-by-step guide and template, landlords upload information, photos, and pricing details for the space. To determine when tenants can rent, landlords simply select the availability of the space on a month-to-month basis. (Oh, and best of all: there’s no cost to list a space.)

Feel secure by knowing exactly who you’re renting to.

PadPiper confirms all students are legitimate by verifying their school email addresses. Landlords can access students’ profiles to see their education, work experience, and previously rented PadPiper spaces.

Request and receive electronic payments for initial fees.

Landlords can require first month’s rent and/or security deposit. Through Stripe on PadPiper, students electronically pay these fees to the landlord before moving into the space.

Easily communicate with tenants throughout the entire process.

Landlords and students can communicate with each other to discuss details of a lease and send documents through PadPiper’s messaging portal.
